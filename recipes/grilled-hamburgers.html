Grilled Hamburgers

A great classic to any households dinner. A nice juicy, smokey grilled hamburger, that allows you add your favorite topping in between the buns. This quick, satisfying, low calorie, high protein dinner, perfect for any meat eater.

ingreidents

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon liquid smoke flavoring
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • seasoned salt to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill for high heat
  2. In a medium bowl, lightly mix together the ground beef, worcestershire sauce, liquid smoke and garlic powder. Form into 3 patties, ahndling the meat minimally. Brush both sides of each patty with some oil, and season with seasoned salt.
  3. Place the patties on the grill grate, and cook for about 5 minutes per side, until well done.
